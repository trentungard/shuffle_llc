our story

st. neumann hall, freshman year, myles penny was watching a youtube series titled, "deconstructed", in his room with his roommate chase campbell. this series broke down some of their favorite songs by their favorite artist. it was from these videos that myles became dissatisfied with the music industry and the way music was being handled. to gain a better understanding of the music industry, myles reached out to his friend lukas radulski, who was was an experienced producer and happened to live down the hall from myles in st. neumann hall. after talking with lukas about the ongoing problems in the music industry, both lukas and myles began to formulate ideas about how to conquer these problems. Shuffle. was born that night at la salle university in neumann 115.
